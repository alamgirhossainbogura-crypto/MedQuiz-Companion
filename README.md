# 🩺 MedQuiz Companion

MedQuiz Companion is an AI-powered interactive workspace designed specifically for medical and nursing students. Built for the OpenAI Build Week Hackathon, this platform solves the challenge of mastering complex healthcare terminologies and experiencing realistic clinical environments before entering real-life hospitals.

---

## 🌟 Key Features

### 1. 🎭 Patient Simulator
An interactive clinical simulation tool where students can text-chat with a virtual patient powered by GPT-5.6. The AI acts as a patient presenting specific symptoms (e.g., chest pain, high fever), allowing students to practice history-taking, vital checks, and diagnostic workflows in a safe environment.

### 2. 📚 Study Simplifier
Medical literature can be overwhelming. This feature allows students to input complex jargon or drug information and leverages OpenAI Codex and GPT-5.6 to instantly break it down into easy-to-understand summaries and creative mnemonics for better retention.

### 3. 📝 NCLEX Mock Test Generator
Generates high-yield, scenario-based multiple-choice questions aligned with NCLEX standards. It provides instant clinical rationales explaining the core physiology behind correct and incorrect answers to accelerate exam preparation.

---

## 🛡️ Important Note
> **NB:** This application is powered by AI for educational and training purposes only. It must not be used for real-life medical diagnoses or clinical decision-making.
